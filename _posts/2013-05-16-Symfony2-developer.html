About us

The Team

Bodaclick IT team members are the responsible for the maintenance and delivery of new features and products in the web site & application for all the countries we are in. We are all passionate developers, frontenders and sysadmins who work to its best for our users.

You will be part of one of the smaller Scrum teams, working closely to another 2 or 3 people, working with github and github-like workflow.
